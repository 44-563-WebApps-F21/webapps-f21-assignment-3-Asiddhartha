Bad lucker (Rebecca 'Becky' Sharpe)

nicknamed Hazard by Cisco Ramon and designated as Subject Two by Clifford DeVoe, was a meta-human with the power to spread bad luck on people whilst giving herself good luck. She was briefly an ally of Team Flash until her body and powers were taken by Clifford DeVoe.
